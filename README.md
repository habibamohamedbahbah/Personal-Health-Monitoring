# Personal Health Monitoring

## Project Overview
**Personal Health Monitoring** is an interactive web-based platform designed to support users in monitoring and improving their physical and mental health.  
The platform provides personalized guidance, exercises, and health suggestions based on the user’s condition.

A key focus of the project is supporting **people with special needs**, especially individuals who rely on **sign language**.  
The system ensures full accessibility by offering health content adapted for sign language users.

## Key Features

### 1. Smart Health Assistant
A built-in chat-based assistant that allows users to:  
- Describe their physical or mental health symptoms  
- Receive personalized treatments or exercise recommendations  
- Get guided steps to improve and monitor their condition  

### 2. Sign Language Support (Accessibility)
To support individuals with hearing impairments, the platform includes:  
- Sign language video explanations for exercises and health tips  
- Health information presented in a simple visual format  
- An accessible interface compatible with assistive technologies  

This ensures users who rely on sign language can understand, interact, and benefit fully from the system.

### 3. Physical & Mental Health Guidance
The platform covers both:  
- **Physical Health:** exercises for back pain, neck pain, migraines, fitness routines  
- **Mental Health:** stress relief tips, breathing exercises, emotional support strategies  

### 4. User-Friendly Interface
Designed to be:  
- Simple  
- Clean  
- Accessible  

---

## Project Goal
The main objective is to provide an **inclusive, AI-assisted health support system** that helps users—especially those with special needs—access reliable health guidance in a smooth, interactive, and accessible way.
